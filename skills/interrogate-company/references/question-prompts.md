# Starter Questions for Competitive Interrogation

Present these grouped by category at session start. The user can pick one or ask freely.

---

## Differentiation & Positioning

- "How can I differentiate myself from them?"
- "Where is my positioning strongest against theirs?"
- "What messaging angles could I use to win against them?"
- "What do they claim to be the best at that I could credibly challenge?"
- "How are we fundamentally different in approach, not just features?"

---

## Weaknesses & Gaps

- "What are their biggest weaknesses?"
- "What customer segments are they underserving?"
- "What complaints do their customers likely have?"
- "Where does their product have obvious gaps?"
- "What does their pricing model punish customers for?"

---

## Strategic Direction

- "What are they likely to do next?"
- "What markets or segments are they moving into?"
- "Are they moving upmarket, downmarket, or both?"
- "What does their recent hiring tell us about their strategy?"
- "What partnerships or integrations are they building toward?"

---

## Winning Deals

- "How do I win a deal when a prospect is comparing me to them?"
- "What's the strongest argument for choosing me over them?"
- "What objections will prospects have after seeing their pitch?"
- "How should I price against them?"
- "Which of their customers are most likely to churn, and why?"

---

## Market Opportunity

- "What whitespace are they leaving open?"
- "What trends are they not responding to?"
- "What would it take for them to enter my core market?"
- "Where could I win on speed or simplicity that they can't match?"

---

## Content & Narrative

- "What story are they telling the market?"
- "What topics are they owning in content that I should contest?"
- "How is their brand perception different from mine?"
- "What are they saying to enterprise vs. SMB audiences?"
